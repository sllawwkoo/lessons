Three Delicious Recipes for Any Occasion

Italian Pasta with Pesto

Italian pasta with pesto is a classic dish that is quick and easy to make.

Fresh basil, cashews, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil give the sauce a unique flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine)
  • Fresh basil
  • Cashews
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions:

  1. 1.Cook the pasta until al dente.
  2. 2.While the pasta is cooking, prepare the pesto: in a blender, combine basil, cashews, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and olive oil until smooth.
  3. 3.Combine the cooked pasta with the pesto and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. 4.Serve hot, topped with additional Parmesan cheese.

Chicken Kiev

Chicken Kiev is a Ukrainian delicacy.

Juicy chicken fillet, breaded and fried with butter, is a treat for any gourmet.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt, pepper
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. 1.Cut the chicken breasts in half, pound them, and season with salt and pepper.
  2. 2.Place a piece of butter inside each chicken breast.
  3. 3.Dip the chicken breasts in egg, then in breadcrumbs.
  4. 4.Fry the chicken breasts in hot oil until golden brown.

Vegetarian Quinoa Salad

This quinoa salad is a light and nutritious dish that is perfect for vegetarians.

Quinoa, vegetables, and nuts give the salad a rich flavor and texture.

Ingredients:

  • Quinoa
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Bell pepper
  • Green onion
  • Nuts (cashews, almonds)
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions:

  1. 1.Cook the quinoa according to package directions.
  2. 2.Dice the cucumber, tomato, bell pepper, and green onion.
  3. 3.Combine the quinoa, vegetables, nuts, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. 4.Add fresh herbs like parsley or dill, if desired.
